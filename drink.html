Top 10 Drinks

What are Drinks?

Drinks are liquids that people consume to stay hydrated , gain energy, and enjoy different flavours. They include water, juices, soft drinks, tea, coffee, and many other beverages made from natural or processed ingredients. Some drinks provide essential nutrients like vitamins and minerals, while others are mainly for refreshment or taste. Staying properly hydrated with drinks is important for maintaining body functions, supporting concentration, and keeping the body healthy throughout the day.
