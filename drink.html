Food

What is food?

Food is what we eat to give our bodies the energy they need to function each day. It supports growth, movement, and overall health. Food comes from sources such as plants and animals, and each type provides different nutrients that the body relies on. These include carbohydrates for energy, proteins for growth and repair, fats for stored energy, and vitamins and minerals that help the body work properly. Eating a balanced variety of foods is important for maintaining good health and ensuring the body gets everything it needs to function effectively.
